Switzerland
Switzerland, officially known as the Swiss Confederation, is a country located in Central Europe and surrounded by the Alps. The capital is Bern, while Zurich and Geneva are the largest cities and main financial centers..
Finland
Finland is located in Northern Europe and borders Sweden to the west, Norway to the north and Russia to the east. The country has more than 188,000 lakes and 179,000 islands, making it one of the countries with the most lakes in the world.
France
France is located in Western Europe and is the largest country in the European Union. The country is bordered by Belgium and Luxembourg to the north, Germany, Switzerland, and Italy to the east, and Spain and Andorra to the south.
Netherland
The Netherlands, located in Western Europe, is a country known for its windmills, tulip fields and sophisticated water management systems. About 25% of its territory is below sea level, making the country one of the places with the world's leading water management technology.
Indonesia
Indonesia is the largest archipelago in the world, consisting of more than 17,000 islands, with over 300 ethnic groups and more than 700 languages. The capital is Jakarta, located on the island of Java, the most populous island in Indonesia.
