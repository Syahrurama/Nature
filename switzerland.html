Switzerland

Switzerland, officially known as the Swiss Confederation, is a country in Central Europe bordered by Germany to the north, France to the west, Italy to the south, and Austria and Liechtenstein to the east.

With an area of about 41,290 square kilometers and a population of about 8.5 million, Switzerland is a country of fascinating cultural and linguistic diversity.

Geography and Nature
Switzerland is widely recognized for its spectacular natural scenery, especially the Alps which cover about 60% of the country's total area.
