Finland

Finland, officially known as the Republic of Finland, is a Nordic country in Northern Europe. It is bordered by Sweden to the west, Norway to the north, and Russia to the east.

With an area of about 338,455 square kilometers and a population of about 5.5 million, Finland is known for its stunning landscapes and high quality of life.

Geography and Nature
Finland is famous for its beautiful lakes and forests, with around 188,000 lakes and vast wilderness areas.
